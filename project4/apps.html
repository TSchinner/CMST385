Calculator

A simple online calculator that offers users a straightforward and intuitive tool for basic arithmetic operations. With its minimalist design and easy-to-use interface, users can quickly add, subtract, multiply, and divide numbers with precision. Whether it's for simple calculations or quick math tasks on the go, this app provides efficiency and convenience, making everyday calculations a breeze.


Random Number Generator

A random number generator offers a straightforward solution for generating random numbers within specified ranges. Its intuitive interface allows users to set minimum and maximum values quickly, providing flexibility for various applications. Whether it's for selecting winners in a contest, generating random passwords, or conducting statistical experiments, this app offers reliability and convenience. With just a few taps, users can access random numbers with ease, making it a handy tool for a wide range of tasks.


Metric to Imperial Converter

The Metric to Imperial Converter app simplifies the process of converting measurements between metric and imperial units. With a clean and user-friendly interface, users can effortlessly convert lengths, weights, volumes, and temperatures with just a few taps. Whether you're traveling abroad or need to work with different measurement systems, this app provides a handy tool for seamless conversions, ensuring accuracy and convenience in everyday tasks.
